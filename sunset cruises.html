Sunset Catamaran Cruises

Enjoy the comfort and amenities of a 50' catamaran to view Santa Barbara sunsets from the Pacific. You may be lucky enough to spot a whale while more common visitors off the decks include dolphin, seals and sea lions. To add to your comfort, full bar onboard as well as snacks for purchase. With reservations made far enough in advance, this experience is available as a private charter.
