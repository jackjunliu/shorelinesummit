Charters are the ultimate in privacy with your family, friends, company or organization. They are available from 1 day to 5 days and 4 nights. The best value (what you pay vs. what you get) is a full charter of 30-35 passengers depending on the boat selected. Also available for about 40% less is a half-full boat of 15-17 passengers.

(note: please visit our store for pricing or keep reading for more information)

For those looking for single day charters, there are adventure specific dives to the outer islands (San Miguel and Santa Rosa), the largest and most serene island (Santa Cruz), the oil rigs in the middle of the Santa Barbara Channel with their “natural” reefs and wreck dives up the coast from Santa Barbara.

Not included in the prices are trips to more distant locales including San Clemente Island, San Nicolas Island, Santa Barbara Island, Santa Catalina Island and the Cortes Bank.

Meals, snacks, non-alcoholic beverages and air fills are included in the price. The typical number of dives is four but can be more or less depending on conditions.
