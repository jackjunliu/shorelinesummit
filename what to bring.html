Diving Equipment:
  • Suba certification card
  • diver's Alert Network (DAN) insurance or equivalent
  • Gear bag
  • Mask
  • Snorkel
  • fins
  • Wetsuit (7mm) or drysuit with hood, gloves and booties (water temerature in the summer is in the mid-60's and in the mid-50's in the winter
  • Wetsuit hanger
  • Tank and Buoyancy Compensator Device (BCD)
  • Weight belt and weights
  • Dive knife
  • Regulator with pressure gauge, depth gauge, compass and decompression computer
  • Surface Marker Buoy (safety sausage)
  • Underwater (U/W) light and Cyalume (chemical) light for night dives
  • Save-a-Dive kit (extra fin strap, mask strap, o-rings, silicon, tools, high and low pressure hose, batteries, bulbs, wetsuit cement, zipper wax, etc.
Personal Items:
  • Dress in layers for both warm and cool weather
  • Low-heeled, non-marking rubber soled shoes for aboard the boat
  • Sleeping bag for extra comfort (bunks are vinyl covered mattresses with pillows and blankets)
  • Hat
  • Sunscreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Swimsuit
  • Towels
  • Personal toiletries (soap, shampoo, toothbrus, toothpaste, etc.)
  • Camera (dry-bag or ziplock bag suggested)
  • Flashlight
  • Alcoholic Beverages (if desired)
  • Seasickness Medicine (take 12 hours prior to and again 30 minutes before departrue if there's a tendency for motion sickness)
